Edible Book Fest

April 12, 2014

South Community Library

Entry forms due April 9.

Schedule of Events

  • 8-9:30 a.m. - Participants drop off edible entries at South Community Library.
  • 9:30-11 a.m. - Judging and photography.
  • 10-1 p.m. - Public voting for Patron's Choice Award.
  • 1:15 pm - Welcome and judges' commentary.

Play with your food!

Create a book-related dish inspired by a favorite tale, a pun on a book title or in the shape of a book made entirely out of edible materials.

Whether you're a professional chef or just like to play with your food, the festival is open to anyone! It's free to enter, but registration is required by Wednesday, April 9.

Non-perishable food donations will be accepted during the Fest for the WAFER community food shelf.

Don’t want to create an entry? Come and vote for your favorite on Saturday, April 12th, from 10 a.m.–1 p.m.

Guidelines: Individuals and groups of any size may submit an entry in the festival. Entries must be made from anything that is edible (cake, bread, crackers, Jell-o, fruit, vegetables, candy, etc.) and must be related to books in either shape or theme.

Your edible entry will not be served. Commercially made treats will be served after the announcement of winners.

Awards: Participants will receive a certificate, and prizes will be awarded in the following categories:

Patron's Choice

Best Pun/Funniest

Most Creative

Best Youth

Best Group

Best Individual

Best Visual Presentation

Best Commercial

Entries will be documented, photographed and displayed at South Community Library and on the library's Flickr and Facebook pages.

Questions? Contact South Community Library, telephone 608.789.7103.
